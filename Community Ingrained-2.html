Welcome to Community Ingrained Wellness

Support for immigrant and refugee families in King County starts here.

Learn More

To make a significant contribution to the social and economic status of our immigrant and refugee families in our community through the effective delivery of culturally relevant programs and services. Everyone should be able to access high-quality culturally appropriate programs and services regardless of ethnicity, race, religion, or sexual orientation.Our goal is to empower our community by educating them on the various opportunities available to them and create a resilient community that is prepared to navigate the social and legal challenges in the United States.

Our Programs

At Community Ingrained Wellness, we are committed to supporting youth and families through programs designed to provide guidance, mentorship, and essential resources.

iLead Youth Intervention Program

iLead is a youth-focused initiative that empowers young individuals from low-income communities or those facing academic struggles. Through mentorship, career development, and academic support, iLead helps youth build a strong foundation for future success.

PACS System Navigation

Our PACS System Navigation program assists African families in South Seattle with navigating essential systems, including Seattle Public Schools, the criminal justice system, and social services. By providing guidance on standardized testing, teacher communication, and court processes, we empower families to become self-sufficient and overcome common challenges such as language barriers.
